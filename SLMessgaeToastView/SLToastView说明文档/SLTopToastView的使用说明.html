SLBottomToastView使用说明

功能说明：模仿IOS原生底部弹出框，进行相关信息展示

使用实例：

  2. //头文件引入
  3. #import "SLBottomToastView.h"
  4. #import "FactoryLeftCell.h"
  6. 实现相关协议SLBottomMessageBoxDataSource, SLBottomMessageBoxDelegate

在监听和相应事件中初始化 SLBottomToastView

  1. SLBottomToastView *bottomToastView = [[SLBottomToastView alloc] init];
  2. bottomToastView.dataSource = self;
  3. bottomToastView.delegate = self;
  4. [self.view addSubview:bottomToastView];
